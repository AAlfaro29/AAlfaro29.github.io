All about me

I was born in Bogota (Colombia) in 1976. My father is a civil engineer and owns an engineering company for the last 45 years. I always enjoy travelling but I never have in my plans to live in another country like Australia. I finished a bachelor’s degree in industrial engineering, and I arrived in 2002 to study my masters in Logistics and Operations Management at the University of Wollongong. Australia became my home since then I have enjoyed every moment and opportunity that this great country has offered me. I’ve got two beautiful daughters and I have lived in Peru and Saudi Arabia due my husband’s work. I have lived and travelled to beautiful places and I truly enjoy different cultures!.
